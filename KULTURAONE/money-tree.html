About
Money Tree is HollywoodB unleashed. No filters. No pretending. This is the version of me that doesn't care about fitting into anyone else's expectations. It's confidence, hunger, and ambition at full volume. At its core, the song is about chasing your dreams with everything you have and refusing to let doubt, criticism, or anyone standing in your way dictate your path. It's the mindset of betting on yourself, embracing success without apology, and letting your work speak louder than opinions. There's an undeniable flex throughout the record but it's a calculated one. Every bar is a statement that I know where I'm going, and I'm not asking for permission to get there.
