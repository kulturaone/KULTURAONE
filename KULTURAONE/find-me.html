About
Find Me stands as the climactic, high-energy apex of the 2023 album NEW FEELING, serving as the final, defiant heartbeat of the b3aver alter-ego. Originally conceptualized as a standalone single for someoneyouknew, the track was repurposed as a vital narrative bridge, narrowly escaping the cutting room floor to become a cinematic, synth-heavy anthem defined by driving, powerful drums. As the second last track on the album, it acts as the last uptempo track, marking the definitive conclusion of the b3aver era. Though rumors swirl regarding a new project, this track remains the final transmission of an identity that has permanently evolved; discover the ending to find the new.
